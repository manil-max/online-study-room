# Community Guidelines — Odak Kampı

**Version:** 1 · **Date:** 2026-07-17  
Acceptance is required before generating content (chat, group names, avatars, etc.).

## Prohibited

- Hate, harassment, threats, sexual exploitation, illegal content  
- Spam, fraud, impersonation  
- Sharing others’ private data without consent  
- Attacks / abuse of the platform  

## Expected

- Respectful communication  
- Study-focused classrooms  
- Safe handling of invite codes and groups  

## Reporting & blocking

In-app report/block paths when enabled. In emergencies contact local authorities.

## Enforcement

Warning, content hide, temporary ban, account termination. Appeal via in-app feedback.
