Delete your Odak Kampı account

In-app (preferred): open the app → Settings → Account → Delete account. You will re-enter your password. Deletion is scheduled with a 14-day cooling-off window; you can cancel in the app before the deadline.

If you uninstalled the app: reinstall, sign in, and use the same path — or contact the developer email listed on the store listing with the subject “Account deletion” from your account email.

Hard deletion removes account data per our privacy policy and retention schedule after the grace period (server job).

TR: Ayarlar → Hesap → Hesabı sil. 14 gün içinde iptal edilebilir. Uygulama yoksa mağaza geliştirici e-postasına hesap e-postandan yaz.
